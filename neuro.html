Neurology Department

About the Neurology Department

Our hospital's neurology department is dedicated to the diagnosis, treatment, and management of neurological conditions affecting the brain, spinal cord, and nervous system. Our team of experienced neurologists, neurosurgeons, and other healthcare professionals work together to provide comprehensive care for our patients.

Our neurology services include:

  • Neurological consultations and evaluations
  • Diagnostic testing, including electroencephalography (EEG), electromyography (EMG), and nerve conduction studies
  • Treatment of neurological disorders, including epilepsy, stroke, multiple sclerosis, Parkinson's disease, and Alzheimer's disease
  • Neurosurgery, including brain and spinal cord surgery
  • Neurorehabilitation, including physical and occupational therapy
  • And more
