Gastroenterology Department

About the Gastroenterology Department

Our hospital's gastroenterology department is dedicated to diagnosing and treating diseases and disorders of the digestive system, including the esophagus, stomach, small intestine, colon, rectum, pancreas, liver, and gallbladder.

Our gastroenterology services include:

  • Endoscopy
  • Colonoscopy
  • Flexible Sigmoidoscopy
  • Liver Biopsy
  • Upper Gastrointestinal (GI) Series
  • And more
